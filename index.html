Data Driven Coffee Packaging

CAFE GRANJA LA ESPERANZA IS A HIGH-ALTITUDE COFFEE FARM IN COLOMBIA, KNOWN FOR ITS DISTINCT FLAVOR PROFILES. THIS PACKAGING TRANSLATES KEY DATA (ALTITUDE, HUMIDITY, TEMPERATURE AND MORE) INTO DESIGN ELEMENTS THAT REFLECT THE COFFEE’S SENSORY EXPERIENCE. THE RESULT IS A PACKAGING SYSTEM THAT TELLS THE STORY OF THE COFFEE’S ORIGIN AND MAKES ITS TASTE INSTANTLY RECOGNIZABLE.
