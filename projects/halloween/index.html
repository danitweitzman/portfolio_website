Halloween 1978 Title Sequence

2024

Motion, Graphics

A redesigned opening sequence inspired by Kazland’s Welcome to Shadow Land. This version strips horror down to its core—sharp shapes, bold contrasts, and eerie typography. Classic imagery is broken into fragmented symbols that move through space but still feel flat and graphic. Slow, creeping motion builds tension, interrupted by sudden shifts, mimicking the film’s psychological horror.

Motion: AfterEffects
Graphics: Figma, Illustrator
