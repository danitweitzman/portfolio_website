Inside The Dangerous, Secretive World of Extreme Fishing

2024

Code, Editorial

This project transforms The Atlantic’s article into an immersive reading experience. As users scroll, the screen darkens, revealing text only through a moving beam of light—mimicking the tension of navigating deep, dangerous waters. Designed to translate tone into interaction, it uses motion, darkness, and visibility to shape how the story is experienced.
